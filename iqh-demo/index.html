What Is This??

This is the Impossible Quiz H Edition Demo,
a preview of an upcoming H: The Series
game made by BCMGF1137/19. This
will include questions from the
full game, so you might want to study up!

Rules

There are multiple answers,
and only one is the correct answer.
Watch out for tricks, by the way!

Lives don't exist here. Therefore,
you will have to restart immediately
upon getting a wrong answer!

Bombs

Bombs exist as timers, to
keep you on edge. If its timer runs out,
you lose on the spot.

They range from a relatively harmless
15 seconds to a diabolical 2 seconds.
